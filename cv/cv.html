CV

WORKS

His work explores the environments and conditions under which
images are produced and circulated, with a particular interest in the
subtle contexts behind visual forms. He often begins by tracing unseen or
background materials to generate something new.
Since 2023, he has been studying Visual Communication Design and Art Theory
at Hongik University. His focus spans across graphic design, web, video,
typography, installation, 3D, performance and art direction.
Proficient in developing visual concepts and articulating them
through diverse visual languages, he is deeply engaged in creating images
that have never existed before.
