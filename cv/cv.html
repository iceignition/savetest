Kutahn is an image maker based in Seoul.
He primarily creates, deconstructs, and synthesizes images.
His work explores the idea that subjects are perceived as
singular images within an image space and from this,
he aims to create "easy-to-eat" visuals.

Kutahn explores the environments and conditions
under which images are produced and circulated.
He is particularly interested in the subtle contexts
behind visual forms. He often begins by tracing
unseen or background materials to generate
something new.

Since 2023, he has been studying Visual
Communication Design and Art Theory at
Hongik University. His focus spans graphic design,
web, video, typography, installation, 3D,
performance, and art direction.

Proficient in developing visual concepts and
articulating them through diverse visual languages,
he is deeply engaged in creating images
that have never existed before.
